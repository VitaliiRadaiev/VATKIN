Colours of Freedom
War

In 2022, Ukraine finds itself at the center of historical events, defending freedom, independence, and the value of a democratic society. Warrior heroes defend blue skies and yellow fields every day, giving their lives for freedom and the right to their existence.

Not without reason, Pantone has defined the Ukrainian flag as the color of freedom in 2022, where blue means freedom and yellow means a thirst for life.
